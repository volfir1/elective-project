School History

Sun Valley National High School, established in Sun Valley, Parañaque City, has been a cornerstone of quality education in the community. Since its founding, we have dedicated ourselves to providing excellent academic opportunities to our students.

Today, SVNHS stands as one of the leading public secondary schools in Parañaque, offering comprehensive junior and senior high school programs that prepare students for higher education and future careers.

Why Choose SVNHS

At Sun Valley National High School, we pride ourselves in providing:

- Quality education aligned with DepEd standards
- Comprehensive academic programs for both Junior and Senior High School
- Modern facilities and learning resources
- Dedicated and experienced faculty members

Our Approach to Learning

We believe in holistic education that develops not just academic excellence but also character and life skills. Our teaching methodology combines:

- Interactive and engaging classroom instruction
- Hands-on laboratory work and practical training
- Extra-curricular activities and leadership opportunities
- Values formation and character development
