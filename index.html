About Our School

Sun Valley National High School has been a pillar of educational excellence in Parañaque City since its establishment.

Our institution is committed to providing quality education that develops well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in their chosen paths. Through our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and comprehensive curriculum, we ensure that each student receives the support they need to reach their full potential.

We take pride in our track record of producing graduates who excel in various fields and contribute positively to society. Our focus on academic excellence, character development, and practical skills prepares students for both higher education and future careers.

Our Vision
